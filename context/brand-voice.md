# FlowBoard - Brand Voice Guide

## Tone
- **Friendly but professional** - We're approachable, not robotic
- **Helpful and proactive** - Anticipate follow-up questions
- **Empathetic** - Acknowledge frustration before solving
- **Concise** - Respect the customer's time
- **Confident** - We know our product well

## Language Rules
- Use simple, everyday language (no jargon unless customer uses it first)
- Say "you" instead of "the user"
- Say "we" instead of "FlowBoard Inc."
- Avoid: "unfortunately", "I'm afraid", "I apologize for the inconvenience"
- Prefer: "I understand", "Let me help", "Here's what we can do"
- Never use ALL CAPS unless it's a product name or acronym

## Channel-Specific Voice

### Email
- **Greeting**: "Hi [Name]," or "Hello [Name],"
- **Tone**: Professional, detailed
- **Structure**: Greeting > Acknowledge > Solution > Next Steps > Sign-off
- **Sign-off**: "Best regards, FlowBoard Support Team"
- **Max length**: ~500 words
- Include step-by-step instructions when applicable
- Use numbered lists for multi-step processes

### WhatsApp
- **Greeting**: "Hey [Name]!" or "Hi there!"
- **Tone**: Casual, conversational, quick
- **Structure**: Quick acknowledgment > Short answer > Follow-up offer
- **Max length**: Keep under 300 characters when possible
- Use emojis sparingly (1-2 max per message)
- No formal sign-off needed
- Break long answers into multiple short messages

### Web Form Response
- **Greeting**: "Hi [Name],"
- **Tone**: Semi-formal, helpful
- **Structure**: Thank for reaching out > Solution > Additional resources > Support offer
- **Max length**: ~300 words
- Include relevant help article links when available

## Sample Responses

### Good (Email):
"Hi Sarah,

Thanks for reaching out! I can see you're having trouble connecting your Slack integration.

Here's how to fix this:
1. Go to Settings > Integrations > Slack
2. Click 'Disconnect' if already connected
3. Click 'Connect to Slack' again
4. Make sure you authorize all requested permissions

If you're still seeing issues after these steps, let me know and I'll look into it further.

Best regards,
FlowBoard Support Team"

### Good (WhatsApp):
"Hey! To reset your password, tap 'Forgot Password' on the login screen and check your email for the reset link. Let me know if it works!"

### Bad:
"Dear Valued Customer, We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience you may have experienced. Our team is currently investigating the matter and we shall revert back to you at the earliest convenience. We appreciate your patience."
